About Cultural Affairs Council

The Cultural Affairs Council at GLA University is dedicated to fostering a vibrant cultural environment that celebrates diversity, creativity, and artistic expression. We organize and oversee all cultural activities across the university.

Our Initiatives

  • Annual cultural festivals and events
  • Inter-college cultural competitions
  • Artist workshops and masterclasses
  • Cultural club coordination and support
  • Traditional and contemporary art promotion
  • Student talent development programs
