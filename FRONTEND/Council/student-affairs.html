Student Affairs Council

Dedicated to enhancing student life and academic experience at GLA University.

About Student Affairs Council

The Student Affairs Council at GLA University serves as the bridge between students and the administration, working tirelessly to ensure that every student's voice is heard and their needs are addressed.

Our Responsibilities

  • Student representation and advocacy
  • Academic policy recommendations
  • Campus life improvement initiatives
  • Student grievance resolution
  • Organizing student welfare programs
  • Facilitating communication between students and faculty
