Ninad Club

Ninad, the music hub of GLA University, is a family of passionate performers and music lovers. From stage performances to soulful jams, the club offers therapeutic escape, skill-building sessions, and showcases exceptional talent across genres including classical, Bollywood, rap, and instrumental.
