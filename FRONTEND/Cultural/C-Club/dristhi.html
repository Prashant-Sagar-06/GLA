DRISHTI CLUB

"Vision Beyond Boundaries - Creative Expression Unleashed"

Drishti Club is GLA's dynamic cultural team dedicated to creative expression through events, design, media, and innovation. We are passionate individuals committed to student empowerment through artistic endeavors, visual storytelling, and cultural celebrations. Our club focuses on nurturing creative talents, organizing spectacular events, and providing a platform for students to showcase their artistic abilities and vision.
