LITERARIO CLUB

"Where Words Come Alive - Crafting Stories, Building Dreams"

Literario Club is the literary heart of GLA University, dedicated to fostering a love for language, literature, and creative expression. We provide a platform for budding writers, poets, and literary enthusiasts to explore their creativity, share their work, and engage in meaningful discussions about literature. Through poetry sessions, storytelling events, book clubs, and writing workshops, we nurture the literary talents of our members and promote the rich tradition of literature in all its forms.
