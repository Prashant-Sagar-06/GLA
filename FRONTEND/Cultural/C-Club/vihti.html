VIHTI CLUB

"Preserving Heritage - Celebrating Traditions - Embracing Culture"

Vihti Club is dedicated to preserving and promoting traditional arts and cultural heritage among students at GLA University. We organize cultural festivals, traditional art workshops, and heritage events. Our club serves as a bridge between the past and present, helping students connect with their roots while appreciating the rich cultural diversity of India. Through various activities, we ensure that traditional art forms, customs, and values continue to thrive in the modern academic environment.

Annual Heritage Festival 2024

Grand celebration of Indian cultural heritage with traditional performances and exhibitions.

Traditional Folk Art Workshop
