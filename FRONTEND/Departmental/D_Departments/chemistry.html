Chemistry Department

Discover the fascinating world of chemistry through our comprehensive programs that combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on laboratory experience.

About Department

The Chemistry Department at GLA University offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers all major areas of chemistry including organic, inorganic, physical, and analytical chemistry. Our students gain a deep understanding of chemical principles and their applications in various fields.

The department is equipped with modern laboratories and sophisticated instruments to provide hands-on experience in chemical analysis, synthesis, and research, preparing students for careers in industry, research, and academia.

Programs Offered

  • B.Sc. in Chemistry
  • M.Sc. in Chemistry
  • Ph.D. in Chemistry

Key Features

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering all branches of chemistry
  • Well-equipped modern laboratories
  • Experienced and qualified faculty
  • Research opportunities in various fields
  • Industry collaborations
  • Career guidance and placement support
