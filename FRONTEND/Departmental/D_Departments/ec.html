Electronics & Communication Engineering

Explore the frontiers of electronics and communication technology with our comprehensive curriculum and state-of-the-art facilities.

About Department

The Electronics & Communication Engineering department at GLA University offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application. Our students learn about electronic circuits, communication systems, signal processing, and emerging technologies in the field of electronics and communication.

The department is equipped with modern laboratories and facilities to provide hands-on experience in various aspects of electronics and communication engineering, preparing students for successful careers in industry and research.

  • Ph.D. in Electronics & Communication Engineering
