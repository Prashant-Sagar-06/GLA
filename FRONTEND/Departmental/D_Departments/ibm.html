Information Technology & Management

Bridge technology and business expertise with our comprehensive IT management programs designed for the digital era.

About Department

The Information Technology & Management department at GLA University offers comprehensive programs that combine technical expertise with business acumen. Our curriculum focuses on IT management, software development, system administration, and digital business strategies.

Students learn to bridge the gap between technology and business, preparing them for leadership roles in IT organizations and tech-driven enterprises.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • MBA in Information Technology Management
  • Certificate Programs in IT Management

Key Features

  • Industry-relevant curriculum with latest technologies
  • State-of-the-art computer labs and infrastructure
  • Expert faculty with industry and research experience
  • Strong industry partnerships and internship opportunities
  • Focus on emerging technologies and digital transformation
  • Excellent placement support and career guidance
