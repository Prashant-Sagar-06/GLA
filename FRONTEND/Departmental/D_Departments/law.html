Law Department

Advance legal education and justice through comprehensive legal studies, preparing future legal professionals for a dynamic legal landscape.

About Department

The Law Department at GLA University offers comprehensive legal education programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical application. Our curriculum covers various areas of law including constitutional law, criminal law, corporate law, international law, and emerging areas of legal practice.

The department is committed to developing ethical legal professionals who are equipped to serve society and contribute to the advancement of justice and legal reform.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.)
  • Bachelor of Arts + Bachelor of Laws (BA LL.B.) - 5 Year Integrated
  • Bachelor of Business Administration + Bachelor of Laws (BBA LL.B.) - 5 Year Integrated
  • Master of Laws (LL.M.)
  • Ph.D. in Law

Key Features

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering all major areas of law
  • Experienced faculty with academic and practical expertise
  • Moot court and legal aid clinic for practical training
  • Industry partnerships and internship opportunities
  • Focus on legal research and publication
  • Strong placement support in legal firms and judiciary
