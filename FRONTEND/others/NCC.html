About NCC at GLA University

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at GLA University is dedicated to fostering discipline, leadership, and patriotism among students. Through a variety of training programs, camps, and social initiatives, NCC cadets develop essential life skills and contribute to nation-building.

Our cadets participate in annual training camps, adventure activities, and community service projects, gaining exposure to teamwork and leadership in real-world scenarios.

The NCC unit at GLA University has a proud history of achievements, with cadets excelling in various state and national level events. The program instills a sense of responsibility, discipline, and commitment to the nation.

Join NCC at GLA University to embark on a journey of self-discovery, service, and leadership!
