About NSS at GLA University

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at GLA University is committed to developing the personality of students through community service. NSS volunteers actively participate in various social initiatives, awareness campaigns, and outreach programs to make a positive impact on society.

Our volunteers engage in activities such as blood donation camps, cleanliness drives, health awareness programs, and rural development projects, fostering a spirit of social responsibility and teamwork.

The NSS unit at GLA University has a strong record of service, with students contributing to both local and national causes. The program encourages leadership, empathy, and a commitment to community welfare.

Join NSS at GLA University to be a part of meaningful change and personal growth through service!
